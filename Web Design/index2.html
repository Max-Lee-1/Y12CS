Pina Colada Dome

by Hannah Brebner

Brief

Indulge in tropical paradise with the exquisite Pina Colada-inspired multilayered dessert. A coconut base, with a tangy pineapple compote, inside a cloud-like coconut mouse. Topped with a chocolate coconut shell to let this essence of summer dance on your palate. Each bite is a getaway to the tropics, an Escape to the serenity of sipping a pina colada.

Ingredients

Coconut mouse:
• ¼ c water
• 2 leafs of gelatine
• 90 ml coconut milk
• 90 ml coconut cream
• 180 ml whipped cream
• 30g sugar

Pineapple compote:
• ½ pineapple
• 100g sugar
• 90 g pineapple juice
• ½ a lemon juiced
• 15 g Cornstarch
• 2 tbsp water + ¼ C
• 1 leaf of gelatine

Base: • ½ cup flour
• ¼ cup brown sugar
• ¼ cup coconut (1/2 coconut flakes and dedicated coconut)
• 70g butter melted

Steps

Coconut mouse:
1. Pour the cold water into a bowl. Sprinkle the gelatine into the cold water. Set aside, let sit for about 5 minutes.
2. Heat the coconut milk, cream of coconut and pinch of salt. Heat until just warm, Add the gelatine and stir until all the gelatine is dissolved. Allow to cool to room temperature.
3. In mixing bowl pour the heavy whipping cream and add the white sugar. Beat until fluffy and soft peaks are forming.
4. Carefully add the coconut mixture to the whipped cream and stir until combined.
5. Place in refrigerator. Allow to cool and set for at least 4 hours.

Pineapple compote:
1. Dice up pineapple to uniformed pieces
2. In a sauce pan over medium heat combined this pineapple, sugar, lemon juice, and pineapple juice.
3. Simmer until sugar has dissolved. Then cook for 10 minutes
4. Mix in a separate bowl the water and cornstarch. Then soak the gelatine leaf in the other water
5. Add cornstarch to pineapple mixture and cook until thick for an extra 5
6. Then add gelatine
7. Set in fridge until set

Base:
1. Combine dry ingredients, then add melted butter. Stir until it forms a dough/breadcrumbs
2. Roll out and cut with a cookie cutter
3. Bake at 180 degrees for 5-10mins

Mango Lassi Multilayer Dessert

by Alexia

Brief

Indulge in the perfect harmony of flavours and textures with my Mango Lassi Multilayer Dessert. Immerse yourself in the tropical delight of juicy mango compote, exquisitely layered over a light and fluffy sponge cake base. The velvety richness of whipped yogurt complements the vibrant sweetness of the mango, creating a symphony of taste that dances on your palate. Each spoonful is a journey through layers of culinary artistry, blending the timeless appeal of traditional mango lassi with a modern twist.
